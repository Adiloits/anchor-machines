Anchor Machines is your intermediary partner that associates with equipment owners and users, using a digital-enabled platform to enable a seamless process of hiring and sourcing quality and functional equipment.

Why Us..

With over five years of experience in the industry, we have experienced the challenges our clients and partners face in struggling to source the equipment yet, on the other hand, asset owners have them idle and only work a quarter of the time they are able to.

This shows the grievances on either side due to the failure to have the right information. In addition, the parties deal with challenges of brokers and mistrust while tracing equipment, among others.

We have set out to improve the processes and activities of hiring out your equipment or sourcing the right ones specific for your site assignment needs in that instant. We are providing services aided by a digital platform using a web and mobile app that allows access and use of all our services at your convenience inclusive of management of transit and payment. Imagine that!

Our Services

Equipment Hiring

At the press of a button contractors and site managers can access the equipment within their locality inclusive of special details and prices at their convenience in real-time. We provide functional, quality, and the right equipment for the job our clients specify.

Leasing Equipment

We source and connect various equipment owners through our platform to increase their visibility on the market in turn to improve the possible equipment work time. Using the equipment owner platform one can register and update data about the ones to lease out for an income.

Community

We are committed to building an environment that empowers all users while protecting their rights as they engage in the community guidelines.
